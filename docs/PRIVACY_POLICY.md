# Privacy Policy - AUN Task Counter

Last updated: 2026-03-12

AUN Task Counter ("the Extension") is a browser extension that displays task progress on AUN (aun-mypage.tools).

## Data Collection

The Extension does **NOT** collect, store, or transmit any personal data or browsing information. All processing occurs entirely within your browser.

## What the Extension Accesses

The Extension reads task status information (open, in-progress, done) from the DOM of aun-mypage.tools pages to calculate and display progress in an overlay widget. This data is used solely for on-screen display and is never sent to any external server.

## Clipboard Access

When you click the copy button, the Extension writes progress text to your clipboard. This action is user-initiated only.

## Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Any updates to this policy will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
